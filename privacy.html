Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 15, 2025
Developer: Alex Depp
This Privacy Policy applies to the Android applications Battery Health Monitor and Privacy Guard – Phone Security (the “Apps”).

Summary

  • Local processing only: the Apps analyze device information directly on your phone.
  • No personal data collected by us: we do not collect, store, or sell personal data on our servers.
  • No data shared by us: we do not share personal data with third parties.
  • Third-party services: ads and purchases are handled by Google services under their own policies.

Information We Collect

We do not operate backend servers to collect personal information. The Apps function using information available on your device and process it locally.

Information Processed Locally on Your Device

The Apps may access and process the following information locally:

  • Battery Health Monitor: battery level, charging status, temperature, voltage, and related battery metrics provided by Android.
  • Battery Health Monitor (optional): app usage statistics (foreground activity time) when you grant “Usage Access” permission.
  • Privacy Guard: installed applications, granted permissions, permission changes, and locally generated privacy reports.
  • Local app data: settings, onboarding status, alerts, reports, and historical data stored only on your device.

This information is used solely to provide the Apps’ features such as insights, reports, notifications, and shortcuts to Android system settings.

Data Storage

All data generated by the Apps is stored locally on your device. You may clear local data at any time using the in-app “Clear local data” option or through Android system settings.

Data Sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties because we do not collect or store it on our servers. However, the Apps may use Google services for advertising and in-app purchases, which may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

Third-Party Services

The Apps may include the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob / Google Mobile Ads (advertising in the free version)
  • Google Play Billing (subscriptions and purchase restoration)

These services may collect device identifiers and related information as required to provide ads, prevent fraud, and process purchases.

Permissions

The Apps may request Android permissions only to enable specific features:

  • Notifications: to deliver alerts you enable.
  • Usage Access (Battery Health Monitor): to analyze app usage activity time.
  • App visibility / package access (Privacy Guard): to list installed apps and show permission exposure.

You can deny or revoke permissions at any time in Android system settings. Some features may be limited if permissions are not granted.

Children’s Privacy

The Apps are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect data stored locally on your device. Please note that no method of storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

  • You may disable ads by purchasing a subscription, if available.
  • You may clear local app data at any time.
  • You may revoke permissions via Android system settings.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
